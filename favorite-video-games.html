Favorite Video games

Factorio

Might be my favorite game, the factory building is great, but what really gets me going is the train system and how you can build a whole automated train system and then just watch them go. It's a pleasure that I haven't gotten from any other game. Once I have my train system up, setting up a new stop to bring over iron and then the train just magically will appear because that's how the system works is awesome. Even just watching the trains go around is so cool.
